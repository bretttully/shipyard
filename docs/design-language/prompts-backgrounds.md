Produce **three variations of a single image — the wide title hero** (the "background_2" scene below), in the **dark** scheme, presented together as options 1, 2, and 3. **Phase 2 — the full set. Only after I pick an option.**

## The rules (firm)

- **Cool teal dominates; exactly one warm coral focal point** in the whole composition. Everything else is teal and neutral.
- **No text, no words, no letters or numbers, no logos** in any image — the site overlays titles itself. Leave the specified area clear for that overlay.
- Deep, calm, nautical-industrial. Low-key cinematic lighting, generous negative space, matte finish, subtle film grain. Precise, engineered, seaworthy.
- **Not** neon, not cyberpunk, not distressed, not a purple-to-blue gradient, no lens-flare clutter.
- **Two schemes, both first-class.** The dark scheme is a shipyard at night. The light scheme is a genuinely higher-key **"dawn at the dry dock"** — pale overcast sea-mist sky, pale steel-teal water, teal structure kept, the single coral beacon still the one warm point. It is **not** a colour inversion and **not** a bright sunny day.

## The look, in words

*Dark:* an abyssal midnight teal-black background (deep blue-green, near `#071A1C`), deep-water teal panels, cool harbor-light teal instrument glow (`#4FB8B0`), lit by a single warm beacon-coral light source (`#FF7F5E`), with faint sonar phosphor-green highlights (`#4DE8A6`) used sparingly, and moonlit off-white for any light shapes.

*Light ("dawn at the dry dock"):* a soft sea-mist grey-teal sky and pale steel-teal water (near `#EEF2F1` to `#CBD8D6`), cool harbor teal structure and glow (`#1F9E9E`), lit by a single warm coral beacon (`#EE6C4D`) that stays the one warm point against the cool light ground; muted, low-saturation, mist in the air.

## The images

### Wide title hero — *(this is the Phase 1 look-book subject)*

A calm wide harbour seen from just above the waterline; a working shipyard with cranes and a docked vessel sits far on the **right** third, lit by cool teal work-lights, with a single small coral beacon low on the right. The entire **left and lower** area is open, uncluttered water and sky — deliberately empty for a title and logo to be placed later. Painterly-photographic. Aspect ratio **21:9**.

### Delivery-model hero (the two-tempo idea, from `brand.md` §4)

A large glowing nautical chart table raised on gantry legs over a small dry dock, seen from a high 3/4 aerial. On the lit chart, faint teal coastlines and plotted routes and a single warm coral waypoint. Small identical vessels move along dashed teal wake-lines out from under the table toward open water — a disciplined fleet. Deep still water all around as negative space. Painterly-photographic. Aspect ratio **4:3**.

### Low-contrast section backdrop (text sits on top)

A very low-contrast wide backdrop: a distant shipyard skyline of cranes and dock lights sitting low on the horizon across still water, heavy mist flattening everything, no focal subject and no single dominant light. Deliberately quiet and even so text can sit on top anywhere. Painterly-photographic. Aspect ratio **16:9**.

### The immutable gate (dramatic hero option)

A single massive sealed lock gate at the mouth of a dark harbour, seen straight-on and low. Three thin parallel beams of cool teal light travel across the water toward the gate and converge to one small glowing coral point at its centre — three things becoming one commit. Still water, wide cinematic frame, the coral point the sole focus, empty sky above for a title overlay. Painterly-photographic. Aspect ratio **21:9**.

### The agent fleet (section image)

A disciplined formation of several small identical vessels crossing deep water, all heading the same way toward one distant harbour marked by a single coral beacon on the horizon. Each vessel carries one small cool-teal lamp; the only warm light in the frame is the far beacon. 3/4 aerial view, calm wake lines, lots of open water as negative space. Painterly-photographic. Aspect ratio **16:9**.

### Social / OG card background

A calm wide harbour with the shipyard low on one side and a single small coral beacon; the centre and one side left completely open and uncluttered for a wordmark and tagline to be composited later. Aspect ratio **1200:630**. Dark scheme only.
